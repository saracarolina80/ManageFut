Football Team Management Platform

Are you a football coach and want to simplify the internal
organization and communication of your amateur sports group?

This app is for you!

Our Funcionalities

Online Chat

Online chat to pass important information between the players and the coach. Forget the multiple social networks when all the information can be passed on in a single place.

Calendar and Schedulling

Insert the date and time of training sessions and games, so that everyone has access to this information.

Statistics tracking

As a coach you can put all the information and statistics about any player, so they can see their performances in the season.
